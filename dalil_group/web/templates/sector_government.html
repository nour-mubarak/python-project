The Challenge

Citizen Trust

Citizens expect consistent, fair, and culturally appropriate responses in both Arabic and English. Any bias, inconsistency, or insensitivity damages trust in public institutions.

Regulatory Compliance

Government AI, especially in GCC, must meet SDAIA governance requirements, fairness standards, and audit trails. Evaluation is not optional—it's mandatory.

Multilingual Equity

Arabic-speaking citizens deserve the same quality of service as English speakers. Yet most AI systems are tested primarily in English, leaving Arabic performance unknown.

Cultural Sensitivity

Public sector AI must respect religious practices, social norms, and regional customs. Inappropriate or stereotypical responses can harm citizens and institutions.
