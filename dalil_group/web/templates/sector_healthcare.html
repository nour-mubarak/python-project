The Challenge

Patient Safety

Medical errors across languages can be fatal. AI symptom checkers and treatment advisors must perform equally well in Arabic and English.

Medication Accuracy

Hallucination or inconsistency in medication guidance is unacceptable. Dosage, interactions, and warnings must be precise in both languages.

Cultural Competence

Health beliefs, prayer times, dietary practices, and family dynamics vary across Arabic-speaking regions. AI must respect these differences.

Regulatory Compliance

Healthcare AI/ML must meet FDA, ICO, and local health authority standards. Audit trails and evaluation documentation are mandatory.
